    Nuqui

    It is reached by air from Quibdó or by sea from Bahía Solano. Traditional Chocoano village surrounded by beautiful beaches lined with tropical rainforest, with comfortable cabins facing the sea, with a wonderful panorama and dives between the jungle, surrounded by small streams of fresh water.

    Capurgana

    Below I will tell you how you can get to Capurgana and I will give you a small introduction of wonderful things you can do to enjoy this wonderful destination.

    Located in the Colombian Caribbean, Capurganá is an ideal destination for rest and rapprochement with nature. The main attraction of Capurganá is that it allows tourists to disconnect from the bustle of cities, because in this town there are neither cars nor motorcycles. The only means of transport are bicycles, boats and carts pulled by horses. It has a wide variety of beaches and natural attractions that serve as a setting for hiking, cycling or horseback riding in the middle of a humid jungle, by the sea and crossing the Capurganá River repeatedly. The most visited sites of this region that belongs to the municipality of Acandí -Chocó- are the waterfall of the sky, the pool of the gods, shark cape and the beaches El Aguacate and Soledad. Another attraction that has been gaining strength is the blow hole of Iracas de Belén, which you can go by bicycle.

    Capurganá

    When it comes to breathing pure air and soaking up virgin nature, sea and sky; Capurganá is one of the best options that Colombians and foreigners may have, as its varied natural wealth offers visitors a wealth of ecological and nautical opportunities, all appropriate for those who, for one reason or another, wish to isolate themselves for a few days from the hustle and bustle. and hustle and bustle of big cities.
